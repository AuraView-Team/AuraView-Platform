Alert system

Three levels. Always clear.

AuraView uses a simple traffic-light system so any team member understands the situation instantly — no training required.

Safe
The location is within normal limits. Crowd count is comfortably below the threshold. No action needed.
Trigger: Count < 60% of limit
Heads Up
Crowd is growing toward the limit. A warning notification appears so you can prepare — not panic.
Trigger: Count is 60–99% of limit
Action Needed
The threshold has been crossed. A full alert banner fires, toast notification appears, and SMS is sent to your team.
Trigger: Count ≥ limit
